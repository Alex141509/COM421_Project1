Realistic Research: University, Web-based, and Local Resources

This lesson is for students starting research in their first semester of the TCID, IA, or Ph.D. in Technical Communication programs. It offers strategies for developing and improving research skills, ensuring more efficient research.

Content Areas

This lesson covers acquiring research resources in these areas:

  • Getting Started
    • Learn to access IIT library services
    • Basic functionality of the IIT library website
  • Research Timelines
    • Formulate a timeline
    • Use it to determine how to obtain resources
  • Requesting Resources
    • Request articles and books from other libraries
  • Local Libraries
    • Access local libraries
    • Know when to use them over interlibrary loan
  • Finding Resources on the Web
    • Review web-based research tools

After reading the sections, take a short quiz to reflect on the strategies.
